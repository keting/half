# Code Of Conduct

[English](#english) | [简体中文](#简体中文)

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
For the full reference text, see <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.

## English

### Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in HALF respectful, inclusive, and
productive for contributors with different backgrounds, experience levels, and
communication styles.

### Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback.
- Focusing criticism on ideas, code, docs, and project decisions rather than on
  people.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

### Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, insults, or sustained disruption.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Personal or political attacks unrelated to the project.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that maintainers reasonably consider inappropriate in a
  professional open source setting.

### Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing acceptable behavior.
They may remove comments, Issues, Discussions, PRs, or other contributions that
violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when necessary.

### Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers
privately through the repository owner's GitHub profile or another private
channel listed by the project. Reports will be reviewed and handled with
appropriate confidentiality.

## 简体中文

### 我们的承诺

我们致力于让 HALF 的公开协作对不同背景、经验水平和沟通风格的贡献者保持
尊重、包容和高效。

### 期望行为

有助于形成良好社区环境的行为包括：

- 使用友好、包容的语言。
- 尊重不同观点和经验。
- 建设性地提出和接受反馈。
- 将批评聚焦在想法、代码、文档和项目决策上，而不是个人身上。
- 对其他社区成员保持同理心。

### 不可接受行为

不可接受行为包括：

- 骚扰、恐吓、侮辱或持续干扰协作。
- 性化语言或图像。
- 与项目无关的人身或政治攻击。
- 未经明确许可公开他人私人信息。
- 维护者合理认为不适合专业开源协作环境的其他行为。

### 执行

维护者负责解释和执行本行为规范。对违反本规范的评论、Issue、Discussion、
PR 或其他贡献，维护者可以进行删除、隐藏、关闭，或在必要时临时或永久限制
相关人员参与项目。

### 报告

如果你经历或观察到不可接受行为，请通过仓库所有者 GitHub 主页或项目列出
的其他私下渠道联系维护者。维护者会在适当保密的前提下审查和处理报告。
